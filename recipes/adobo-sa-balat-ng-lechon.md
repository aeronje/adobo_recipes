# Adobo sa Balat ng Lechon 🐖🔥

**Branch:** `feature/lechon`

- 500 g leftover lechon skin  
- 30 ml soy sauce  
- 25 ml vinegar  
- 1 tbsp calamansi juice  
- 3 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 small red onion, diced  
- 1 tsp peppercorns  
- 1 tbsp oil  
- Small pinch rock salt  
- Optional: 1 tsp brown sugar for glaze
